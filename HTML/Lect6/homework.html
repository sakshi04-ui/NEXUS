Weekly Gym Workout Plan

  • Monday - Chest Day
  • Tuesday - Back Day
  • Wednesday - Shoulder Day
  • Thursday - Biceps Day
  • Friday - Leg Day
  • Saturday - Triceps + Cardio/Abs

Monday - Chest Day

  1. Barbell Bench Press - 4 sets of 8 reps
  2. Incline Dumbbell Press - 4 sets of 10 reps
  3. Cable Chest Fly - 4 sets of 12 reps
  4. Push-Ups - 3 sets of 15 reps
  5. Chest Dips - 3 sets of 10 reps
  6. Machine Chest Press - 3 sets of 12 reps

Tuesday - Back Day

  1. Deadlifts - 4 sets of 6 reps
  2. Pull-Ups - 4 sets of 8 reps
  3. Barbell Rows - 4 sets of 10 reps
  4. Seated Cable Row - 3 sets of 12 reps
  5. Lat Pulldown - 3 sets of 10 reps
  6. Hyperextensions - 3 sets of 12 reps

Wednesday - Shoulder Day

  1. Overhead Barbell Press - 4 sets of 8 reps
  2. Dumbbell Lateral Raises - 4 sets of 12 reps
  3. Arnold Press - 4 sets of 10 reps
  4. Cable Lateral Raises - 3 sets of 12 reps
  5. Front Dumbbell Raise - 3 sets of 10 reps
  6. Machine Shoulder Press - 3 sets of 12 reps

Thursday - Biceps Day

  1. Barbell Curl - 4 sets of 10 reps
  2. Incline Dumbbell Curl - 4 sets of 12 reps
  3. Preacher Curl - 3 sets of 10 reps
  4. Hammer Curl - 3 sets of 12 reps
  5. Concentration Curl - 3 sets of 10 reps
  6. Cable Curl - 3 sets of 15 reps

Friday - Leg Day

  1. Squats - 4 sets of 8 reps
  2. Leg Press - 4 sets of 10 reps
  3. Lunges - 3 sets of 12 reps
  4. Leg Curl Machine - 3 sets of 10 reps
  5. Leg Extension Machine - 3 sets of 12 reps
  6. Calf Raises - 3 sets of 15 reps

Saturday - Triceps Day + Cardio/Abs

Triceps Exercises

  1. Close-Grip Bench Press - 4 sets of 8 reps
  2. Cable Triceps Pushdown - 4 sets of 12 reps
  3. Overhead Triceps Extension - 3 sets of 10 reps
  4. Dips - 3 sets of 12 reps
  5. Skull Crushers - 3 sets of 10 reps
  6. Triceps Kickbacks - 3 sets of 12 reps

Cardio

  1. 15 minutes of Running or Treadmill
  2. 10 minutes of Cycling

Abs Exercises

  1. Plank - 3 sets of 1 minute
  2. Crunches - 3 sets of 15 reps
  3. Leg Raises - 3 sets of 12 reps
  4. Russian Twists - 3 sets of 20 reps

Remember Good Body means Good Life
